Send Money to Pay Suppliers in China

When sending money to pay suppliers in China, the right exchange rate can increase your profit margins and save you thousands of dollars. 

China is packed with numerous manufacturing hubs making it an integral part of the global supply chain and digital economy. In fact, many shipping containers regularly make their way from Chinese ports to Canadian shores as companies restock their inventory. According to globalEDGE, China is number two on Canada’s list of Top 10 import countries. 

So, as goods are imported into Canada from China, it naturally follows that many Canadian companies need to send money to Chinese suppliers. Given the large-scale and long-term nature of these transactions, finding the best exchange rates is key to profitability. 

Why the USD/CAD or CNY/CAD exchange rate matters?

Most supplier payments from Canada to China are made in US dollars, so the USD/CAD exchange comes into play with each transaction. However, the local currency, the yuan (¥), is increasingly becoming popular, so the CNY/CAD also matters.  

Securing the best available exchange rate means your money is worth more in USD or Yuan. China has been dubbed the “World’s Factory,” so it’s no surprise that many Canadian companies want a piece of the pie.  

These companies can save thousands of dollars individually and millions collectively each year by reducing the cost of large-amount FX exchange conversions.  

Here’s a quick example and eye-opener: If you purchase a shipment of goods worth $50,000 USD from China, an exchange rate that’s 5% better than traditional banks saves you $2,500 and gives you more purchasing power.

FAQs

Who can use the MTFX payment service?
Individuals and businesses who need to send money in foreign currency internationally can use MTFX’s services. The beneficiary of the transfer must have a bank account for the funds to be paid into. Personal clients usually use our services to transfer money between their own accounts in two different countries. Business clients usually use our services to transfer funds to suppliers, fund international operations, or repatriate overseas earnings.
Why should I use MTFX and not my own bank?
MTFX offers currency exchange rates that are 2-5% better than those offered by the banks. Personal clients usually save hundreds of dollars per transfer and for larger transfers, the savings can run into the thousands. We also offer excellent customer service, dedicated currency specialists, and a 24/7 online platform with best-in-class technology that allows you to complete transfers from any device virtually anywhere in the world. Business customers save with better currency exchange rates and proven solutions geared towards managing and mitigating foreign exchange risk. Our solutions include forward contracts, market orders, rate alert services, and much more - all backed by great technology and great people.
How do customers send funds to MTFX?
Funds can be transferred via wire transfer, Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT), or ACH payment services. MTFX maintains bank accounts in all major currencies with highly-rated banks. Our banking infrastructure ensures that you can transfer funds to us quickly and securely.
How long does it take MTFX to transfer funds?
