Send Money to New Zealand

If you’re sending money from Canada to New Zealand, it’s crucial to find NZD/CAD competitive exchange rates that leave you with less out-of-pocket. 

There are many Canadian expats living in New Zealand. If you have also decided to make a life among the Kiwis and plan to enjoy New Zealand’s abundant sunshine, that’s a good reason to send money from Canada to New Zealand.  

Other reasons include paying for tuition, mortgage payments for a home in New Zealand, sending money to family and friends, and paying for travel or business investments. Regardless of your reason for moving money to New Zealand, it’s essential to find a competitive exchange rate that keeps your costs to a minimum.  

Usually, a specialist money transfer service is your best bet compared to banks if you want better value when converting your Canadian dollars to New Zealand dollars. 

How to send money to New Zealand from Canada

When you need to transfer money to a New Zealand bank account, using a bank might seem like the most convenient option. However, if you want a better deal that combines low exchange rates, reasonable fees, and quick turnaround times, the smart choice would be to use a specialist foreign exchange service. 

For instance, MTFX is a more cost-effective choice that offers bank-beating rates to save you 3-5% per transfer. If you’re converting $30,000 Canadian dollars, you can expect to save between $900-$1,500, and your savings increase significantly if you need to make regular transfers.  

Sending money through such a service makes even more sense because you have access to many tools and experts that help you streamline your international payments processes.

What information do I need to send money to New Zealand from Canada?

Besides your personal or business details, here’s what you need to make an international money transfer to NZ from Canada: 

  • Beneficiary name 
  • Beneficiary contact details

  • Bank name 
  • Bank address 
  • Bank Swift code 
  • Reason for your transfer

FAQs

Who can use the MTFX payment service?
Individuals and businesses who need to send money in foreign currency internationally can use MTFX’s services. The beneficiary of the transfer must have a bank account for the funds to be paid into. Personal clients usually use our services to transfer money between their own accounts in two different countries. Business clients usually use our services to transfer funds to suppliers, fund international operations, or repatriate overseas earnings.
Why should I use MTFX and not my own bank?
MTFX offers currency exchange rates that are 2-5% better than those offered by the banks. Personal clients usually save hundreds of dollars per transfer and for larger transfers, the savings can run into the thousands. We also offer excellent customer service, dedicated currency specialists, and a 24/7 online platform with best-in-class technology that allows you to complete transfers from any device virtually anywhere in the world. Business customers save with better currency exchange rates and proven solutions geared towards managing and mitigating foreign exchange risk. Our solutions include forward contracts, market orders, rate alert services, and much more - all backed by great technology and great people.
How do customers send funds to MTFX?
Funds can be transferred via wire transfer, Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT), or ACH payment services. MTFX maintains bank accounts in all major currencies with highly-rated banks. Our banking infrastructure ensures that you can transfer funds to us quickly and securely.
How long does it take MTFX to transfer funds?
Our global network of banking partners allows us to get funds to virtually anywhere in the world quickly and efficiently. Most wire transfers from MTFX will be received by your beneficiary within 24-48 hours. MTFX also offers same-day transfers that are almost instantaneous, as well as low-cost in-country payment services for your less urgent transfers.
